**Effective date:** 2026-06-14

This Privacy Policy describes how Sweeply ("we", "us", "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile app.

## 1. Who we are

Sweeply is an independent app made by an independent developer based in Norway. You can reach us at **emilsmielak.tor@gmail.com** for any privacy questions.

## 2. Information we collect

When you create an account and use Sweeply, we collect:

- **Account info:** email address, username, password (stored hashed by Firebase Authentication — we never see your raw password), display name (optional), bio (optional), profile photo (optional).
- **Recordings:** the audio you record, the peak decibel level of each recording, the animal you picked, timestamps, and whether you submitted it to the weekly "weirdest" feed.
- **Social data:** your friends list, friend requests sent and received, upvotes you give, comments you post, users you've blocked.
- **Reports:** if you report a recording, we log the recording ID, your user ID, and the timestamp.
- **Device info:** device platform (iOS or Android), provided when you use the Feedback button.
- **Country (optional, opt-in per recording):** when you tick "📍 Tag this clip with my country" before saving a recording, we ask your device for your approximate location, convert it to a country **on your device**, and save only the country code (e.g. "NO") and country name (e.g. "Norway") with that recording. **Your exact coordinates are never sent to our servers, never stored, and never shared.** Tagging is off by default and chosen per recording.

We do **not** collect: precise location (we only derive country on-device, see above), contacts list, browsing history, advertising identifiers, or biometric data. We do **not** use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or trackers.

## 3. How we use it

- To run the core features of the app (record, leaderboards, friends, comments, voting).
- To prevent abuse (blocking, reports).
- To respond to your feedback emails.

We do **not** sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for profiling.

## 4. Where your data is stored

- Account info, friends, comments, upvotes, blocks, and reports are stored in **Google Firebase** (Firestore + Authentication). Firebase is operated by Google and may store data on servers in the United States and other regions. See Google's Firebase privacy documentation for details.
- Audio recordings and profile photos are stored on **Cloudinary**. Cloudinary may store data on servers in the United States and other regions.

We do not have direct access to your raw password (Firebase handles authentication).

## 5. Who can see your data

- **Your username, display name, bio, profile photo, and recordings** are visible to all signed-in Sweeply users via the leaderboards and your public profile.
- **Country tags on recordings** (if you opted in) are visible to all signed-in users alongside the recording.
- **Your friends list** is visible only to you.
- **Friend requests** are visible only to you and the recipient.
- **Comments** are visible to all signed-in users who can view the recording they belong to.
- **Blocked users** are visible only to you. Users you block are not notified that you blocked them.
- **Your email address** is not shown to other users. It is only used for authentication, account recovery, and the optional feedback email.
- **Reports** are private — only the developer can read them.

## 6. Children

Sweeply is rated 12+ and is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has created an account, please contact emilsmielak.tor@gmail.com and we will delete the account.

## 7. Your rights

You can:

- **Change your password** via Profile → Account → Change password.
- **Delete your account** via Profile → Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your user profile, recordings, friends, friend requests, and comments. Some content tied to others (e.g. upvotes you gave on their recordings) may remain as orphaned data.
- **Request a copy of your data** by emailing emilsmielak.tor@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
- **Ask us to delete specific data** by emailing the same address.

Under GDPR (if you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland) you also have the right to object, restrict processing, lodge complaints with your local data protection authority, and request data portability.

## 8. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, the data described in section 7 is removed. Backups may persist for up to 30 days before being overwritten.

## 9. Security

We rely on Firebase and Cloudinary for security at rest and in transit (HTTPS/TLS). We do not store sensitive financial data. No system is 100% secure — if we become aware of a breach affecting your data, we will notify you and take steps to mitigate.

## 10. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the Effective date and notify users in the app. Continued use after a change means you accept the updated policy.

## 11. Contact

Questions? Email **emilsmielak.tor@gmail.com**.
