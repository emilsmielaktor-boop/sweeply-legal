Need help with Sweeply? You're in the right place.

## Contact

For any question, bug report, or feedback, email us at **emilsmielak.tor@gmail.com**.
We try to reply within a few days. Please include:

- What happened (or what you'd like to do)
- Your username in the app
- The phone model and OS version (e.g. "iPhone 14, iOS 18")

You can also tap **Send feedback** on your Profile screen — it opens an email with these details prefilled.

## Frequently asked questions

### What is Sweeply?

Sweeply is a fun animal-sound competition app. You record your best animal impression, get a score, and compete with friends and the world on two leaderboards:

- **Loudest** — how loud your impression is
- **Weirdest** — how weird the world finds it (voted by upvotes each week)

### How old do I need to be?

You must be at least **13 years old** to use Sweeply. The app is rated 12+ on the App Store.

If you are under 18, please make sure a parent or guardian knows you are using the app.

### How do I add a friend?

Go to your **Profile** tab. Tap **Add a friend** and enter their exact username. They will get a friend request and can accept or ignore it.

You can share your own username by tapping it on your Profile (it copies to the clipboard).

### How do I delete a recording?

Open your Profile, then tap **View & manage your clips**. Tap the trash icon next to any recording you want to delete. You can also delete every recording at once with the big red button at the bottom.

### How do I block someone?

Open their profile (tap their name anywhere in the app) and tap **Block this user**. Blocked users disappear from leaderboards and comments, and any friendship between you is removed.

### How do I report a recording?

Tap the small warning icon (⚠️) on any recording in the Weirdest feed, or long-press the row. Reports are reviewed by us manually.

### How do I delete my account?

Open your Profile → **Account settings** → scroll down to **Delete account**. You will be asked to type DELETE and re-enter your password. This permanently removes your profile, recordings, friends list, and friend requests.

### I can't sign in. What now?

- Use the **Forgot password?** link on the sign-in screen to reset.
- If your email never receives a reset link, check your spam folder.
- Still stuck? Email **emilsmielak.tor@gmail.com**.
